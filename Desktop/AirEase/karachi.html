Welcome to "PIA" Pakistan Int Airport


It is Pakistan's largest airline with a fleet of more than 30 airplanes.PIA is going through a procedure of privatization to shift management from government to the private sector.It employed nearly 14,000 people as of annual report 2015 and the airline has overall punctuality of 88%

PIA serves 22 domestic and 28 international destinations in 28 countries across Asia, Middle East, Europe and North America. Dubai is a major focus city for the airline with flights from Dera Ghazi Khan, Islamabad, Karachi, Lahore, Multan, Peshawar and Quetta.

It was the second Asian airline to acquire a jet aircraft, a Boeing 707. More recently, it was the launch customer of the Boeing 777-200LR.[7] The airline also owns The Roosevelt Hotel and Sofitel Paris Scribe Hotel.[8]
