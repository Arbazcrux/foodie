Flights to Your Destination Featured Destination

Airlink operates daily, direct flights to Durban from Bloemfontein, George, Maputo and Nelspruit. Durban’s sub-tropical climate makes it the perfect destination at any time of the year. Its beautiful beaches are a must for all surf and sea enthusiasts and the city also boasts a state of the art football stadium as well as world class shopping malls. Durban truly embodies the ‘Rainbow Nation’ espoused by Archbishop Tutu as it is a melting pot of different cultures. Just a few hours to the north of Durban, Airlink’s passengers can enjoy the beauty of the Drakensberg Mountains, another of South Africa’s ‘World Heritage Sites’.
